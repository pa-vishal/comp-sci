Your Personal Recursion Handbook

Welcome. This site is a distillation of the core concepts required to master recursion, from the foundational "Leap of Faith" to advanced pattern recognition for solving complex problems.

Use the navigation above to explore the key pillars of recursive thinking:

  • The Mindset: Internalize the three non-negotiable rules of recursion.
  • Analysis: Learn to visualize recursion with trees and analyze performance with recurrence relations.
  • Common Patterns: Recognize templates for problems involving DFS, Backtracking, and Dynamic Programming.

True mastery comes from understanding the structure, not from tracing every function call. Let's begin.
